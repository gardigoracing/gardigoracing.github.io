First Track Day

First trackday? Let’s make it a good one. We’ll sort the basics ahead of time, I’ll guide you through the day and we'll focus on the right habits early, so you leave smiling and wanting to come back.

Sim to Track

We’ll map your sim strengths to real-world driving, work through the key differences, and help you connect the real-world sensations to what you already know from the sim. I can also advise which events match your experience level so you can progress quickly.

Driver Improvement

Whether you’re chasing consistency or lap time, I can help in two ways: on-track support during your day (structure, goals, feedback between sessions) and post-session analysis using video/telemetry. The idea is to make each trackday count and keep the progress moving.

Ready to Get on Track?

Whether you're preparing for your first track day, setting up your sim, or looking to improve your driving, get in touch and we’ll figure out the next step together.
